Cantilever System

Deva's Entrance Automation offer cantilever system for gates , some place practically not possible bottom track that time use cantilever system for gate.

A Cantilever Sliding Gate will remove the need for tracking along the length of the opening. A cantilever gate is suspended over the entrance with horizontal posts. Cantilever gates are quieter than the traditional floor track systems.

However due to the nature of the cantilever design an additional section of gate is required to 'hang' over the carriage units, often behind a wall or hedge perimeter. This means that additional room is needed beside the gate opening than a tracked version and may prevent you from choosing this option.

A simple way to look at this system is that you have two carriage units that look like upside down roller skates and a track the runs on these rollers. The gate sits on top of this track.

In summary, do not use a cantilever system unless the ground is uneven, when your sliding gate is running across the ground it won't move or drop.

Rfid Readers Controllers

Automatic Gate Control System using wireless technology (AGCSWT), uses Radio frequency identification (RFID) technology to read the Entry/Exit vehicles & open an automatic motorized barrier. It helps in efficiently managing vehicle traffic flow in residential, commercial and industrial zones & car parks. It is the most popular and cost-effective method to control & monitor vehicle movement for security / control reasons & is a integral part of any Building Management System.

RFID technology using RFID tags, RFID Readers& RFID Antenna has a unique advantage over other conventional tools &brings the latest automation in Wireless technology to the User. It allows easy& Speedy access to anybody who has been identified as secure. The identified object can be a Human being, A vehicle or any Mass or object entering the access control zone

The tags, which has the vehicle identification stored in its memory,are attached to the windshield of authorized vehicles are read by the reader through the antenna within a distance of 5-6meters. Once the reader identifies the vehicle it operates an automatic Barrier which opens & allows the Vehicle to entry the Premise. At the Exit of the Barrier there is a closing loop (option) which closes the Barrier.

Alternatively we can avoid a loop & have a time based function for closing the barrier. This fully automated, hands-free; no waiting operation not only makes the Entry process much simpler and quicker, but improves the efficiency as well. The Software tools can be used to reports on entry/ Exit details on daily or monthly basis
